Dictionary Bookmarklet

  • Check out the source code. Copy it to your clipboard.
  • Add a page to your bookmarks bar.
  • Paste the source code where the bookmark's URL would normally go.
  • Highlight a word then click the bookmark.
  • Voila!
  • + expands the definition window, – collapses it, and × dismisses it.
  • Once the bookmarklet has been activated, press alt + d to define words.
  • Once defined, words remain highlighted. Double click for a reminder.
  • Revel in the definitions!
